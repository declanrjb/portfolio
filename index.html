Hi, I'm Declan Bradley - a student with a passion for data journalism. As an editor of my campus paper, I built open-source tools for visual storytelling and led the newsroom through a high-profile investigation on the mishandling of student data.

Currently, I am a full time college student and student journalist. I also contract as an Automated Webscraper for the Associated Press. My writing on data visualization has appeared in Nightingale. In 2022, I was honored to receive the National Scholastic Press Association's first ever Innovation Pacemaker Award. My visualization of U.S. Congressional campaign finance was named the 2023 Multimedia Story of the Year for interactive graphics by the Associated Collegiate Press.
